CSIT

The B.Tech. Computer Science and Information Technology, an undergraduate programme, is crafted to nurture motivated, innovative, and passionate graduates to fill ICT positions across sectors who can conceptualize, design, analyse, and develop ICT applications to meet modern-day requirements. The B.Tech. in Computer Science and Information Technology curriculum is outcome-based and it delivers the most advanced theoretical concepts and practical skills in the domain. By enrolling on this programme, students develop critical, innovative, and problem-solving abilities for a smooth transition from academia to the corporate world. .
